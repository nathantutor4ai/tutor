Featured AI Tools

Discover some of the most powerful AI‑powered resources for educators and learners. These tools can help you save time, personalize learning and spark creativity.

AudioPen

An AI‑powered web app that turns your voice into polished text. Use it to quickly draft blogs, lesson plans and emails by speaking instead of typing.

Curipod

Create interactive lessons in minutes. Curipod uses AI to generate customized slides and activities—polls, word clouds and SEL check‑ins—tailored to your topic.
